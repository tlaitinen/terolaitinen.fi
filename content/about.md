Hi, I'm Tero. I [work](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tero-laitinen-b6918525/) as a staff engineer at Wolt. Before joining Wolt, I've worked in a family business (Audio Riders Oy), as a doctoral student researching the Boolean satisfiability problem ([Extending SAT Solver with Parity Reasoning](https://aaltodoc.aalto.fi/items/01de330d-6f85-4b65-a394-e80c31e116b4)), as a freelancer (Audience First, Sentina Oy, iProtoXi Oy), and as a co-founder (Underground City Oy, PipeKeeper Oy).

This blog is a creative outlet and a learning journal. I'm currently interested in:
- Exploring the intersection of formal methods and agentic engineering, specifically in detecting and resolving inconsistencies automatically.
- Improving agent orchestration, cost efficiency, quality gates, accuracy and reliability, and collaborative agentic development.
- Exploring bottlenecks in software product organizations when integrating agents into processes.
